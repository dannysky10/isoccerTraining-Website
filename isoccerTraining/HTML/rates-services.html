Individual skills and ability analysis (Approx. 2hrs)

Prior to beginning a training program with IST, it is recommended that the player participate in the individual skills and ability analysis. Results of the analysis gives the trainer the information he or she needs to develop a personalized training program.

Individual Training

Emphasis on skills development (passing, heading, shooting, dribbling), first touch control, offensive/defensive positioning, improved decision making and vision. Call or message to discuss your individual training needs.

Small Group Training

Includes individual skills development, 1 v 1 proficiency, spatial awareness, tactical awareness, first touch control, improved decision making and offensive/defensive positioning. Call or message to discuss your group training needs.

Team Training

Call or message to discuss and for more details.

Additional Services

Video analysis – One of our team members will video record your game/practice. It will be meticulously analyzed in order to discover your strengths and weaknesses. This is a very effective method used to properly develop an individualized training program. Cost $50 - $100. Call or message to discuss.
